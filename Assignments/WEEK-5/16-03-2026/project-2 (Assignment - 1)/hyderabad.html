Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, is a major Indian metropolis known as the "City of Pearls" and a vibrant hub for technology, pharmaceuticals, and history. Founded in 1591, it perfectly blends Nizami heritage—exemplified by the Charminar and Golconda Fort—with modern infrastructure, including the HITEC City IT hub and diverse, popular cuisine like Hyderabadi Biryani.
